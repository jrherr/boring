Touch Records released today a live recording of Philip Jeck on their Touch Radio site.

22.09.11 - Live at The Brücknerhaus – 32:58 - 192 kbps

From the Touch Radio Website: “Philip Jeck in performance was recorded on 6th September 2011 at The Brücknerhaus, Linz, Austria for Prix Ars Electronica, at which Philip Jeck was awarded a Distinction for his LP ‘Suite: Live in Liverpool’.  The multi-channel recording (2 channels off the desk, two in the room) was mixed for TouchRadio by BJNilsen.”
