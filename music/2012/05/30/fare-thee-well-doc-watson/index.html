Sometime in 1998, I drove down to Bristol, Tennessee / Virginia, with some friends to the Birthplace of Country Music.  We were there for the 70th anniversary festival and congressional awarding of the ‘27-‘28 Bristol Sessions, and, most importantly, to see the headlining show by Doc Watson (with grandson Richard).  Probably won’t be too many other shows to knock that one out of my top ten most memorable concerts.  Thanks for the great music, Doc Watson.
